Is my Cume Games account secure?

If you use the same email address and password on Cume as you used on another site which has been compromised, then your account is vulnerable to attack. To secure your Cume account, use a unique password, and enable multi-factor authentication.

Which payment methods are supported?

The Cume Games Store supports credit cards, PayPal, and a variety of alternative payment methods.

Which platforms does the Epic Games Store support?

Epic Games Store now offers cross-platform support. You can check platform compatibility for each game title by referring to the “About the game” section of any product page.
